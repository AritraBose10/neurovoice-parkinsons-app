Voice Biomarker Analysis

Jitter (Frequency Variation)

--

Measures vocal fold instability. Higher values may indicate motor control issues.

Shimmer (Amplitude Variation)

--

Measures vocal fold weakness. Elevated levels may suggest reduced voice control.

HNR (Voice Quality)

--

Harmonics-to-Noise Ratio. Lower values indicate breathier, less clear voice quality.

Pitch Variability

--

Standard deviation of fundamental frequency. Reduced variability may indicate monotone speech.

RPDE (Voice Complexity)

--

Recurrence Period Density Entropy. Higher values may indicate irregular vocal patterns.
